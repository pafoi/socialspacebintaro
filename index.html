ABOUT BINTARO SOCIAL SPACE

Bintaro is an area on the south-western outskirts of Jakarta, consisting of a planned township called Bintaro Jaya and the surrounding area. As an independent "city inside a city", Bintaro has everything; from traditional markets, international schools, hospitals, recreational malls, to hip cafes and hangout places. It is home to the ultra rich as well as the not so fortunate. Offering cleaner air and more relaxed vibe and minimum traffic jams compared to downtown Jakarta, paired with easy access to Jakarta and the surrounding satellite cities through a well-developed toll road network; Bintaro is the perfect place to live, work and play.

Bintaro Social Space is a part of The Social Spaces, a social project involving physical places that enable social activities to take place, with a long term goal to acquire through partnerships, rent and/or purchase properties or spaces in as many countries as possible. Unlike its establishment in other countries such as Philippines and Singapore, The Social Space @Bintaro will not only take place in a single property, but involves the entire Bintaro area, with Eunike Space as its epicenter / basecamp.
